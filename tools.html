Here are the list and description of each Basic Vlogging Tools / Equipments

Mobile phone

First of all mobile phone of course. Here we can start especially
since we are just beginners. Here we will record our videos.

Tripod/ GorillaPod

This will serve as the stabilizer of our video production. You can
attach it anywhere, it is a flexible pod that will guide us to get
a good angle for our video production.

External Microphone

This will help us to reduce the noise around us and we will have
better audio quality because we have it. This tool is also very
important especially if you are fond of outdoor vlogs.

Note: If you want good audio quality, you need to use an external microphone.

Lighting

This is an important tool especially for those who love indoor vlogs,
we will need better lighting so that our viewers can see us better.

Video Editing Software

There are many editing software we can choose from. Using our mobile
phone, we can download editing software in the selected free
downloading store. (Playstore, App Store, AppGallery, and Etc.)
